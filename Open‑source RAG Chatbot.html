Failure in coating and its types

Failure in coating and its types can occur due to several factors, including:

  • Bond breaking: Polymer matrices can weaken due to chemical reactions, mechanical stresses during freeze-thaw cycles, or degradation of the coating's adhesion.
  • Surface defects: Coating degradation leads to issues like deterioration of metal adhesion, development of internal stress, and corrosion.
  • Environmental factors: UV degradation, water and moisture uptake, and chemical damage can all contribute to failure.
